    <h2 class="s-title">The Question</h2>

    <span class="s-label">Research Framework</span>

  </div>

  <div class="q-layout">

    <div>

      <div class="q-big">How does soil<br>metabolize urban<br>transformation?</div>

      <div class="q-body">

        <p>The site around 6002. Cadde is not merely a backdrop to urban development — it is an active participant. Soils accumulate, shift, and respond; they carry the trace of every disturbance while sustaining the conditions for ecological recovery.</p>

        <p>This research asks: what can the soil tell us about the history and future of a landscape? How do species act as indicators of soil health? And how might design work with — rather than against — the metabolic processes already underway?</p>

        <p>The investigation moves between scales: from the molecular chemistry of clay particles to the territorial pattern of road infrastructure; from monthly satellite readings to the lifespan of a reed colony along the Beytepe shoreline.</p>
